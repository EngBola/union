About Us

Union for metal constructions is one of the largest steel manufacturers in the Egyptian market. The company was founded in 2003 by Mr. Ayman Shaltout to trade in steel structures on a large scale, and in 2007 we decided to raise the ceiling of our ambition and invade the steel industry in the Egyptian market.
We have already begun to manufacture steel structures.

We strive to lead the steel manufacturing industry, a full team of experts, Engineers, technicians, and administrators are equipped with the latest machinery to develop you investment and progress together into a better industrial environment.

Therefore, we offer you a cohesive and integrated team to give you 20 years of experience in the form of a wide range of products that meet the requirements of customers, with a production capacity of 30,000 tons per year with a factory area of 20,000 SQM. We seek to gain our client confidence in the latest methods and technology to take your hand to strong experience in the steel structure industry.
